Progress of Abandoned horror

Abandoned horror is a Horror game of the asymmetric genera starring an insane and demonic group of Beasts and victims. This is a 1v6v1 Victim vs Beast vs Assassin game. Abandoned Horror runs on the Unreal Engine 4 and will to be out on steam during the holidays of 2024.

Goals

The CEO of the company started this company in 2016 as a small computer company. We worked on static gaming and never did much until The head dev came to the company and turned it into a functioning one. We here at Imagicast like to be honest and professional with eachother. We value leadership, partner skills, and work to preserve our values to the very end.
