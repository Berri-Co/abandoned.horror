Progress of Abandoned horror

    Abandoned horror is a Horror game of the asymmetric genera starring an insane and demonic group of Beasts and victims.
This is a 1v6v1 Victim vs Beast vs Assassin game. Abandoned Horror runs on the Unreal Engine 4 and will to be out on steam during the holidays of 2024.  






When will this game release?

Abandoned Horror does not have a set release date yet but we are aiming for fall 2023 or 2024.
